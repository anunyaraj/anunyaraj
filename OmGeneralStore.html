Om General Store

 

The shop is a small general or a 'Kirana' store in the vicinity of the Geetapalli colony in Lucknow. Established in 2007, the store sells various products from groceries, toiletries and other household items. The target market is people of all ages since the shop deals with multiple products. The customers are locals residing in the locality and occasional passersby.

The task is to create a brand identity for the shop that resonates with the audience.

 
 
 

MY APPROACH

For the visual identity of the shop, I took inspiration from the spirals present in nature. The retro 'devanagri' type and a retro colour palette consisting of red and yellow gives a retro feel to the brand new identity.
